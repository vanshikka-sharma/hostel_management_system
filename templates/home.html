Your Home Away From Home

Located conveniently inside the campus, our hostel provides a safe and vibrant environment for students.

Our Amenities

Accommodation

We offer two types of rooms: **Single rooms** for privacy and **Dormitory rooms** for a communal living experience. Both are well-maintained and comfortable.

Facilities

Enjoy our **party hall** for celebrations and a dedicated **projector room** for movies and presentations. The hostel also has a beautiful inner **garden**.

Recreation

A wide range of **indoor and outdoor games** are available to ensure you can relax and have fun with your friends. Our vibrant common areas are a hub of activity.

Hostel Life & Events

A Year of Celebrations

Life at our hostel is never dull! We celebrate a variety of **hostel events**, festivals, and special occasions throughout the year, fostering a strong sense of community and camaraderie among residents.

Our Dedicated Team

Our hostel is managed by a team of dedicated professionals who ensure a safe, clean, and healthy environment.

Chief Warden & Warden

Our wardens are available to assist students and ensure smooth operations.

Staff Members

A team of **3 chefs** and **2 cleaning staff** keep the hostel running efficiently.

Security

A dedicated **security guard** and **surveillance cameras** ensure your safety 24/7.

Medical Facility

We provide basic **medical facilities** for any immediate needs of the residents.

Our Unique U-Shaped Building

Our two hostel buildings are uniquely designed in a U-shape, creating a central courtyard that houses our beautiful garden. This layout fosters an open and connected community.
